Game Description

(1) Imagine yourself is below the ground
your vision is the camera on the detector
and the detector is seeking gold underground
(2)When the game start, collect as much
gold as you can
(3) After current goal is accomplished,
the goal will raise
(4) If you could not earn enough money
within 60 seconds, the game will be over
(5) Rock is worth $10 each, small gold $50 each,
medium gold $100 and big gold $150. The value of
a mysterious box is random
